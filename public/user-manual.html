3. How to Log In — Step by Step

📧 Option A — Login with Email & Password
1
Open the Login Page Open your web browser and navigate to the FarsanHub URL. You will see the Login screen.
2
Enter Your Email Address Type your registered email (e.g., admin@farsanhub.com) in the Email field.
3
Enter Your Password Type your password in the Password field. It is hidden as you type.
4
Click the "Login" Button Press the orange Login button.
5
You Are In! You are taken directly to the Create Order page — ready to work.
🔒
Security: After 5 wrong attempts with the same email, the account is blocked for 1 hour. The screen will show how many attempts remain. Wait 1 hour or contact the Super Admin if locked out.
🌐 Option B — Login with Google (Faster)
1
Click "Login with Google" Click the Google button on the Login page.
2
Choose Your Google Account A Google window opens. Choose the account whose email matches your FarsanHub account.
3
You Are Logged In Automatically No password needed — Google confirms your identity and you are taken to the dashboard.
⚠️
Google login only works if your Google email matches an existing FarsanHub account. New accounts cannot be created via Google login.

How to Log Out

Manual logout: Click your name or profile icon in the top-right corner → click "Logout"

Auto-logout: If you leave FarsanHub idle for 5 minutes, a warning popup appears. If ignored for 30 more seconds, you are logged out automatically.

5. Customers Module

Stores all shops and buyers you do business with. Go to Customers in the sidebar.

How to Add a New Customer

1
Click "Customers" in the sidebar
2
Click "Add Customer" button (top right)
3
Fill in the form
  • Customer Name *Person's full name
  • Phone Number *10-digit mobile number (country code stripped automatically)
  • Shop Name *Name of the shop
  • Shop AddressFull address of the shop
  • CityCity name
  • EmailCustomer's email address
  • Latitude / LongitudeFor the customer map pin (get from Google Maps)
  • Customer PhotoPhoto of the person (JPG/PNG, max 5MB)
  • Shop PhotoPhoto of the shop front
  • Status *Active or Inactive
4
Click "Save" — the customer appears in your list

How to Search for a Customer

Use the search box on the Customers list page. You can search by name, phone, shop name, city, or address. Results update automatically.

🗑️
Deleted customers are soft-deleted — they disappear from your list but their order history is preserved for reports.

How to Share a Customer Profile Link

You can send anyone a temporary public link that shows a beautiful profile card for a customer — no login required. The link expires automatically after 10 minutes.

1
Go to Customers in the sidebar and find the customer card you want to share.
2
Click the Share button (share icon) on the customer's action bar.
3
A popup appears with a loading spinner while the link is being generated. It switches to the share link once ready.
4
Use one of the popup buttons — see table below.
5
The countdown bar shows how much time is left. Once it reaches zero the link stops working and the recipient sees an "expired" page.
6
To get a fresh link, simply click the Share button again — the old link is invalidated immediately.
Button What it does
Copy Link Copies the URL to your clipboard so you can paste it anywhere
Open Card Opens the public profile card in a new browser tab
WhatsApp Opens WhatsApp with the link pre-filled in a new message

What the recipient sees

The public card shows:

  • Owner photo & shop photo (tap either to view full size)
  • Customer name, shop name, phone, city, address, email, and GST number
  • Call and WhatsApp buttons linked to the customer's phone number
  • An interactive Google Map with the customer's location pinned (if coordinates are saved)
  • A live countdown showing time remaining before the link expires
🔒
Security notes:
  • Each link expires after 10 minutes — it cannot be used after that.
  • Only you can generate a share link for your own customers.
  • Clicking Share again immediately invalidates the previous link.
  • No login is required to view the card, but the URL is unguessable (48-character random token).

6. Products Module

Manage your product catalog — what you sell or buy, at what price, and in which unit.

How to Add a New Product

1
Click "Products" in the sidebar
2
Click "Add Product"
3
Fill in the form
  • Product Name *e.g., "Khandvi", "Chakli", "Gathiya"
  • Base Price *Default price per unit (used if no customer-specific price is set)
  • Unit *kg or Nang (Displays as કિગ્રા / નંગ in Gujarati)
  • Product ImagePhoto of the product (max 5MB)
  • Status *Active or Inactive
4
Click "Save"

Customer-Specific Pricing

Some customers get a special price for a product, different from the base price. Set it like this:

  1. Go to Products → Edit on the product
  2. Scroll to the "Customer Pricing" section
  3. Choose a customer and enter their special price
  4. Click "Add Price"
✅
Automatic! When you create an order for that customer and select this product, the system automatically uses the correct price — no manual lookup needed.

7. Orders Module

Record every sale (sell order) and every purchase (purchase order).

Order Types

Type When to Use
Sell You are selling products to a customer / shop
Purchase You are buying products from a supplier
Remaining For balance remaining amount in pending payment
Cash For direct cash transactions

How to Create a New Order

1
Choose the Customer Select a customer from the dropdown. The product list will load automatically with that customer's prices.
2
Choose the Product After selecting a customer, choose a product. The correct price (base or customer-specific) is filled in automatically.
3
Fill in the remaining fields
  • Order Date *Pick a date using the date picker
  • Quantity *Can be decimal, e.g., 2.5 kg
  • Price *Auto-filled — can be manually adjusted
  • Order Type *Sell or Purchase
4
See the Live Total As you type Quantity and Price, the Total Amount updates live on screen — no manual calculation needed.
5
Click "Save Order"
⚠️
Duplicate Check: The system warns you if an order already exists for the same customer + product + date. You can still save it if you confirm.

Filtering Orders

On the Orders list, filter by: Order Type Customer Date Range Search

Language Switch

FarsanHub supports English and Gujarati (ગુજરાતી). Click the language toggle in the top header to switch. Your preference is remembered for the session.

Localized Units: When you switch to Gujarati, all units are automatically translated:

  • kg becomes કિગ્રા
  • Nang becomes નંગ

This change applies to all screens, PDF bills, and Excel exports.

Smart Auto-Translation Feature

To make business management easier, FarsanHub includes an Auto-Translation feature. When you add or update a record in English, the system automatically generates its Gujarati equivalent for use in reports:

Module Auto-Translated Fields
🏪 Customers Customer Name & Shop Name
📦 Products Product Name
👤 Users Full Name
✨
Example: If you add a product named Chakli, the system automatically translates and stores it as ચકરી for Gujarati bills.

14. Safety & Auto-Logout

Auto-Logout After 5 Minutes of Inactivity

If you leave FarsanHub open in your browser without doing anything for 5 minutes, a warning popup appears:

"Your session is about to expire. You will be logged out in 30 seconds."

What to do:

  • Click "Stay Logged In" or simply move your mouse / type anything → the timer resets
  • If ignored for 30 seconds → you are logged out automatically
🔒
This protects your business data if you forget to log out on a shared computer.

Login Attempt Protection

  • After 5 failed login attempts with the same email → account blocked for 1 hour
  • Prevents unauthorized people from guessing your password
  • If locked out by mistake → wait 1 hour or ask the Super Admin

15. Suggestions for Improvement

The following additions would make FarsanHub more powerful and user-friendly:

User Experience

1
Forgot Password link on the Login page
Users locked out can reset their own password without contacting the admin.
2
Force password change on first login
New users are created with the default password 12345678. The system should require them to change it immediately — this is a key security gap.
3
Breadcrumb navigation on every page
e.g., Dashboard › Orders › Create — users always know where they are and can go back easily.
4
Quick Add button directly from the Dashboard
A prominent "New Order" button on the dashboard reduces clicks for the most common daily task.
5
Dark mode toggle
Easier on the eyes during long working sessions or in low-light environments.

New Features

6
Customer payment tracking & outstanding dues
Track which customers have paid, what is due, and send reminders. Currently FarsanHub only records orders, not payments.
7
Stock / Inventory module
Track how much of each product is available. Subtract on sell orders, add on purchase orders.
8
WhatsApp / SMS order confirmation
Send customers an instant confirmation message when their order is recorded.
9
Order copy / repeat feature
If the same customer orders the same product every week, one click to copy the last order — saves time on repetitive data entry.
10
Mobile app or PWA (Progressive Web App)
Use FarsanHub on a phone without a browser — add to home screen, works even with slow internet.

Reporting

11
Customer account statement report
A full statement for one customer — all orders, payments, and outstanding balance — useful for month-end collection.
12
Profit & Loss summary
Sell revenue minus purchase costs minus expenses = net profit. Currently these numbers exist but are not combined in one view.
13
Automatic monthly email report
Automatically email a summary report to the owner on the 1st of every month — no manual download needed.

Security & Technical

14
Activity log / audit trail
See who changed what and when (e.g., "Admin A edited order #123 on 15 April"). Important for accountability in a team.
15
Clear error messages for users
When something goes wrong, show friendly guidance ("Please fill in the customer name") instead of technical server errors.
16
Data export / backup feature
A one-click full data export (all orders, customers, products) as Excel so the business owner always has an offline backup.
