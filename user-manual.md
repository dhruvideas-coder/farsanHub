## 1. What is FarsanHub?

**FarsanHub** is an all-in-one business management portal designed specifically for **Brahmani Khandvi & Farsan House**. It replaces paper registers and spreadsheets with a clean, digital system you can use from any browser.

### What you can do with FarsanHub:

| What | Why it helps |
|------|-------------|
| Manage customer profiles | Know exactly who you sell to, where their shop is, and how to contact them |
| Track products & prices | Keep a central catalog with prices per unit (kg / Nang) |
| Record sell & purchase orders | Never lose track of a transaction |
| Set customer-specific prices | Different price for different shops — automatically applied |
| Log business & personal expenses | See where the money goes |
| Generate PDF / Excel reports | Month-end reports at one click |
| View customers on a map | See all shop locations at a glance |

## 2. Who Uses This System?

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|

## 3. How to Log In — Step by Step

### Step 1 — Open the Login Page

Open your web browser and go to the FarsanHub URL. You will see the **Login Screen** with two options:

- Email & Password login
- "Login with Google" button

---

### Step 2A — Login with Email & Password

1. Enter your **Email Address** (e.g., `admin@farsanhub.com`)
2. Enter your **Password**
3. Click the **"Login"** button

**What happens next:**
- If correct → you are taken directly to the **Create Order** page (ready to work!)
- If incorrect → an error message tells you how many attempts remain

> **Security Note:** After **5 wrong attempts**, your account is blocked for **1 hour**. Wait or contact the Super Admin.

---

### Step 2B — Login with Google (Faster Option)

1. Click the **"Login with Google"** button
2. A Google pop-up or page opens
3. Choose your Google account (must match the email on your FarsanHub account)
4. Google confirms your identity
5. You are logged in automatically — no password needed

> **Note:** Google login only works if your Google email matches an existing FarsanHub account. New accounts cannot be created via Google login.

---

### Step 3 — You Are In!

After logging in successfully you will see:
- A **sidebar** on the left with all module links
- A **top header** with your name and logout button
- The **order creation form** ready to use

---

### How to Log Out

**Option 1 — Manual logout:**  
Click your name / profile icon in the top-right corner → click **"Logout"**

**Option 2 — Auto logout:**  
If you leave the browser idle for **5 minutes**, a warning popup appears. If you do not click anything within 30 seconds, you are logged out automatically. (See [Section 14](#14-safety--auto-logout) for details.)

## 4. Dashboard — Your Home Screen

The **Dashboard** gives you a live business snapshot. Access it by clicking **"Dashboard"** in the left sidebar.

### Time Period Filter

At the top of the dashboard, choose a time period:

| Filter | What it shows |
|--------|--------------|
| Today | Only today's data |
| Yesterday | Only yesterday's data |
| This Week | Current week (Mon–today) |
| This Month | Current calendar month |
| This Year | January to today |

All cards and tables on the dashboard update when you change this filter.

---

### The 4 Summary Cards

| Card | What it tells you | Click to go to |
|------|-------------------|---------------|
| **Total Customers** | How many customers you have | Customers list |
| **Total Orders** | Number of orders in the selected period | Orders list |
| **Total Products** | How many products in your catalog | Products list |
| **Revenue** | Total income from sell orders in the period | Orders list |

You can also **filter orders by product** directly from the dashboard using the product dropdown on the Orders card.

---

### Charts

| Chart | What it shows |
|-------|--------------|
| **Revenue Trend (Line Chart)** | Sell & purchase totals for the last 6 months |
| **Top Products (Doughnut Chart)** | Which 5 products sell the most by quantity |
| **Monthly Quantity (Bar Chart)** | How much was dispatched each month |

---

### Recent Orders Table

Shows the **last 8 orders** in the selected period — customer name, product, quantity, rate, total, and date.

---

### Top 5 Customers

A ranked table showing your best customers by total business value. Includes order count, total amount, and breakdown of kg vs. Nang quantities.

## 5. Customers Module

**Sidebar link:** Customers

This module stores all the shops and buyers you do business with.

### How to Add a New Customer

1. Click **"Customers"** in the sidebar
2. Click the **"Add Customer"** button (top right)
3. Fill in the form:

| Field | Required? | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Customer Name | Yes | Person's name |
| Phone Number | Yes | 10-digit mobile number |
| Shop Name | Yes | Name of the shop |
| Shop Address | No | Full address |
| City | No | City name |
| Email | No | Customer's email |
| Latitude / Longitude | No | For map pin (can be set later) |
| Customer Photo | No | Photo of the person |
| Shop Photo | No | Photo of the shop |
| Status | Yes | Active or Inactive |

4. Click **"Save"** — the customer is added to your list

---

### How to Find a Customer

On the Customers list page:
- Use the **search box** at the top — you can search by name, phone, shop name, city, or address
- Results update automatically

---

### How to Edit a Customer

1. Find the customer in the list
2. Click the **Edit (pencil) icon** on their row
3. Change any fields you need
4. Click **"Update"**

---

### How to Delete a Customer

1. Find the customer in the list
2. Click the **Delete (trash) icon**
3. Confirm the deletion in the popup

> Deleted customers are soft-deleted — they won't appear in your list but their order history is preserved.

---

## 6. Products Module

**Sidebar link:** Products

This module manages your product catalog — what you sell/buy and at what price.

### How to Add a New Product

1. Click **"Products"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Product"**
3. Fill in the form:

| Field | Required? | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Product Name | Yes | e.g., "Khandvi", "Chakli" |
| Base Price | Yes | Default price per unit |
| Unit | Yes | Choose **kg** or **Nang** (Displays as **કિગ્રા** / **નંગ** in Gujarati) |
| Product Image | No | Photo of the product |
| Status | Yes | Active or Inactive |

4. Click **"Save"**

---

### Customer-Specific Pricing

Some customers get a special price for certain products (different from the base price). You can set this on the **Product Edit** page:

1. Open **Products** → click **Edit** on a product
2. Scroll to the **"Customer Pricing"** section
3. Choose a customer and enter their special price
4. Click **"Add Price"**

**How it works automatically:** When you create an order for that customer and select this product, the system automatically uses the special price — no manual lookup needed.

---

### Filtering Products by Customer

On the Products list page, use the **"Filter by Customer"** dropdown to see what price each customer would pay for every product.

## 7. Orders Module

**Sidebar link:** Orders

This is where you record every sale (sell order) and every purchase (purchase order).

### Understanding Order Types

| Type | When to use |
|------|------------|
| **Sell** | You are selling products to a customer |
| **Purchase** | You are buying products from a supplier |
| **Remaining** | For balance amount in pending payment |
| **Cash** | For direct cash transactions|

---

### How to Create a New Order

1. Click **"Orders"** in the sidebar
2. Click **"Add Order"**
3. Fill in the form:

**Step 1 — Choose the Customer:**
- Select a customer from the dropdown
- The product list will automatically load products with that customer's prices

**Step 2 — Choose the Product:**
- After selecting a customer, the Product dropdown populates
- The **correct price** (base or customer-specific) is filled in automatically

**Step 3 — Enter Details:**

| Field | Required? | Notes |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Customer | Yes | Select from dropdown |
| Product | Yes | Select after choosing customer |
| Order Date | Yes | Pick a date (date picker opens) |
| Quantity | Yes | Can be decimal e.g., 2.5 |
| Price | Yes | Auto-filled, can be edited |
| Order Type | Yes | Sell, Purchase, Remaining, or Cash |

**Step 4 — See the Total:**
- As you type quantity and price, the **Total Amount** updates live on screen
- No need to calculate manually

4. Click **"Save Order"**

> **Duplicate Check:** The system warns you if you already have an order for the same customer + product + date. You can still save if you confirm it.

---

### How to Filter Orders

On the Orders list page, you can filter by:
- **Order Type** (Sell / Purchase / Remaining / Cash / All)
- **Customer** name
- **Date Range** (from date → to date)
- **Search** by product name, customer name

---

### How to Edit or Delete an Order

- Click the **Edit icon** to change any field
- Click the **Delete icon** to permanently remove an order
- Deletions are permanent — always double-check before deleting

## 9. Reports Module

**Sidebar link:** Reports

Generate and download professional reports for any month.

### Available Reports

| Report | Format | What's included |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Order Report** | PDF | All orders for a customer in a selected month — with summary stats |
| **Expense Report** | Excel | All expenses for the selected month |
| **Customer Report** | Excel | Complete customer directory |
| **Product Report** | Excel | All products with base price and every customer's special price |

---

### How to Generate an Order Report (PDF)

1. Click **"Reports"** in the sidebar
2. In the **Order Report** card, select:
   - **Customer** (required)
   - **Month** (required)
3. Click **"Download PDF"**
4. A PDF opens / downloads with:
   - Company header with logo and address
   - Summary: total orders, customers, quantity, revenue
   - Full order table (product, qty, unit, rate, amount, date)
   - Grand total at the bottom

---

### How to Generate an Expense / Customer / Product Report (Excel)

1. Click **"Reports"** in the sidebar
2. Find the report card (Expense, Customer, or Product)
3. For Expense: select the month
4. Click **"Download Excel"**
5. An `.xlsx` file downloads — open it in Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets

## 13. Account Settings

### How to Change Your Password

1. Click your name or profile icon in the top header
2. Click **"Change Password"** (or find it in the sidebar)
3. Enter your **current password**
4. Enter your **new password**
5. Confirm the new password
6. Click **"Update Password"**

---

### Language Switch

FarsanHub supports two languages:
- **English** (default)
- **Gujarati (ગુજરાતી)**

Click the language toggle in the top header to switch between them. Your preference is remembered for the session.

**Localized Units:**  
When you switch to Gujarati, all units are automatically translated:
- **kg** becomes **કિગ્રા**
- **Nang** becomes **નંગ**

This change applies to all screens, PDF bills, and Excel exports.

---

### Auto-Translation Feature

FarsanHub includes an **Smart Translation** feature to make management easier. When you add or update a record in English, the system automatically creates a Gujarati version for your reports:

| Module | Auto-Translated Fields |
|--------|------------------------|
| **Customers** | Customer Name & Shop Name |
| **Products** | Product Name |
| **Users** | Full Name |

**Example:** If you add a product named "Chakli", the system will automatically store its Gujarati name "ચકરી" for use in Gujarati reports.

## 14. Safety & Auto-Logout

### Auto-Logout After 5 Minutes of Inactivity

If you leave FarsanHub open in your browser without doing anything for **5 minutes**, a warning popup appears:

> *"Your session is about to expire. You will be logged out in 30 seconds."*

**What to do:**
- Click **"Stay Logged In"** or simply move your mouse / type anything → the timer resets and you continue working
- If you ignore it for 30 seconds → you are logged out automatically

**Why this matters:** This protects your business data if you forget to log out on a shared computer.

---

### Login Attempt Protection

- After **5 failed login attempts** with the same email, that account is blocked for **1 hour**
- This prevents unauthorized people from guessing your password
- If you are locked out by mistake, wait 1 hour or ask the Super Admin to reset it
